Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how motivasi ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Application.

1. Information We Collect

The Application is a personal automation tool. It may process:

  • Account or authentication tokens you provide to connect third-party services (e.g. content platforms, media APIs).

We do not sell your personal data.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to operate the Application's features: generating content, producing media, and (where you choose) publishing to connected platforms.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application connects to third-party APIs that have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any service you connect.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication credentials are stored securely within the automation environment used to run the Application. We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

Generated media may be stored temporarily for processing and is removed according to the retention policies of the storage services used.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data associated with the Application by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at moderural@gmail.com.
